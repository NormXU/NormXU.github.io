和式住宅

I'd like to mention the awesome 和式 house where I stayed in 京都. The decoration and furniture in 和式 house is simple and concise. As you can see from the images below, there were only an old television and a small table in the "living room" and no beds were in the "bedroom". What's more, the bathroom was too small to find. It was hard to find any modern techniques in such a 和式 house, even a Wi-Fi router, but the two nights I stayed there were really pleasant. It was a wonderful traditional house in a quiet and convenient neighborhood. All doors were open or closed by sliding. The only entertainment was the old television. But limited channels could be received by this old stuff. Fortunately, we took Nitendo Switch. Tatami mats were a little hard to sleep on, but thanks to futon, it felt not bad to sleep without a bed. Moreover, tatami mats smell well. This 和式 house looked larger than I expected.I would like to live in this house again for next time in 京都. Here is Airbnb Link of this house.





新干线

Since I got used to 高铁 in China, 新干线 seemed to be unattractive to me. However, after my trip with 新干线, I had to say that I was totally wrong. Although China claimed that 高铁 was much faster than 新干线, but 新干线 must have higher average speed than 高铁 does. What's more, passengers can enjoy much larger space than that on 高铁, and even larger than that of first-class seats. Special 便当 were also delicious and cheap. The most important point that made 新干线 stand out of any 高铁s in China was quietness. On 新干线, you will never be bothered by people who play Tiktok videos loudly, unpleasant smell of instant noodles and noisy children and their annoying parents.



On our way from 京都 to 东京, we were lucky enough to see 富士山. It is really a magnificent view.
